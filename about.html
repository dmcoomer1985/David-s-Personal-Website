ABOUT PORTFOLIO HOME

--When I am not working with software developmnent, while maintaining a healthy-work-life balance, I'm doing the following things.

Traveling

One of my favorite things to do when I am not working or producing code is traveling. Some of the places that I have visited consist of New Orleans, Louisiana, Las Vegas, Nevada, Florida, and many others.

When I travel I like to learn about the culture, food, and history of the place I'm traveling to such as in New Orleans. When I travel to Florida it is all about theme parks and the beach! Finally Las Vegas, it is all about the expierence!

One of my expierences in New Orleans was with the famous Ghost tours where you get on a bus and travel to supposedly haunted locations. It is filled with history and I got to see the house from American Horror Story Covenant!

Gaming

Image by gstudioimagen1 on Freepik

What software developer doesn't like to play games? I know this one does! Being in the technology field I have always had a love at heart for gaming.

One of my favorite gaming platforms to game off of is the PlayStation 5. I enjoy playing open world games such as Assassin's Creed Valhalla, and Red Dead Redemption 2.

I think what attracts me to the PlayStation is the rich gaming using a simple controller but integrated with Speakers and vibration. The graphics almost measure up to what some computers are running.

I am not a big fan with computer gaming so I will stick to my PlayStation and it's rich environment.

Movies and Autographs

When I am not gaming and traveling you can find me watching movies and collecting Autographs from movie stars.

I collect movies, and have currently over 200 movies in my collection. Within the last couple of years I started upgrading my technology to 4K.

Since the pandemic I have tried to recreate the theatre expierence in my own home by adding a Sonos soundbar and subwoofer as well as 4K Tv's and player.

I am also in love with how the technology works in the cloud where you can buy movies and then add them using digital codes and have them in a collection to stream which makes it more convinent then disc. I use Vudu or Movies Anywhere so check them out!

I am also proud to say that I collect celebrity autographs and have a few I am particularly proud of. Some of those are John Carpenter, George Lazenby who plays James Bond, and several other Characters from James Bond movies to name a few.
