Things I have built, in no particular order

Movie Guide

For my first project I created a movie guide that displayed posters, and had trailers that would play for each poster. It even had a review section and a search bar where you could search for different movies.

This was created using an API, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS and of course countless hours of hard work.

Another feature that the movie guide had was the ability to search for local listings for movies in an individuals area.

This app would be good for someone who wanted more information about a particular movie they were interested in seeing. I felt like this was the perfect app to Building since I have a passion for collecting movies.

Building a Travel App

The last project that I built was a travel app. It would run on a computer website and was extremely simple. It incorporated a travel planner to help you plan your travels by creating a list for things you need to pack to take with you.

Another feature is it had a search function that allowed users to find hotels, flights, and other travel deals in their desired location.

I got the idea to build a travel app from my love of traveling to different places in the past few years. I realized that there was not alot of websites or applications that had a packing list and a search engine built into it, to help the individual find the best deals.

When I say the best deals we are looking at deals from rental vehicles, flights, hotel air bnb and more.
